Service

One of the most important things in my life is giving back to the people and organizations I'm involved with. Not only is it a great way of expressing appreciation, it also fosters an healthy relationships and communities. Here are a few ways I invest in those around me.

Grace Bible Church

Production Team: 1/19-Present

The biggest responsibility of production team members is to assist in setting up and tearing down the equipment required for each service. Since Grace Southwood's college service takes place in a high school theatre, nothing can stay from week to week and must be reconstructed each time. Aside from this, production team members are also responsible for running lights, sound, and video during the service.

TAMU Hack

Volunteer: 1/19

TAMU Hack is an annual hackathon put on by students at Texas A&M. The hackathon isn't just a time where students can develop their skills and produce a usable product, but a place where recruiters can find potential employees. For this reason, many large companies will invest in TAMU Hack and provide challenges for students to complete. During this time, TAMU Hack volunteers do a number of things, including managing supplies giving to students, troubleshooting technical issues competitors may encounter, and ensuring fitting accommodations for recruiters.
